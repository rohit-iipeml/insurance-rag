**FLOOD COVERAGE ENDORSEMENT**

**Section I – Property Coverages**

**A. Coverage A – Dwelling and Coverage B – Other Structures**

1. **Flood Coverage Addition**
   a. In consideration of the premium specified, the following provisions are added to Section I – Exclusions, paragraph A.3. (Water Damage) of the Policy Form NX-HO-3:
      i. Notwithstanding the foregoing, this Policy is amended to provide coverage for direct physical loss to the Covered Property described in Coverages A and B caused by flood, as defined herein.
      ii. For purposes of this Endorsement, "flood" means:
         (1) A general and temporary condition of partial or complete inundation of normally dry land areas from:
            (a) The overflow of inland or tidal waters;
            (b) The unusual and rapid accumulation or runoff of surface waters from any source;
            (c) Mudslides or mudflows on the surface of normally dry land areas, as these terms are defined under the National Flood Insurance Program (NFIP); or
            (d) The collapse or subsidence of land along the shore of a lake or similar body of water as a result of erosion or undermining caused by waves or currents of water exceeding anticipated cyclical levels.
         (2) The release of water from the failure of a dam or levee, provided such failure is not caused by an act of negligence or willful misconduct of the Insured.

2. **Limits of Liability**
   a. The most Nexus Insurance Company will pay for loss or damage caused by flood under Coverages A and B is the lesser of:
      i. The Coverage A limit shown on the Declarations Page; or
      ii. $250,000 for the Dwelling and $25,000 for Other Structures.

3. **Deductible**
   a. A deductible of $2,500 applies to each flood event. This deductible is in addition to any other deductible shown on the Declarations Page.

**B. Conditions**

1. **Notice of Loss**
   a. The Insured shall provide prompt notice to Nexus Insurance Company of any loss or damage caused by flood, including the date and time the flood began, if known.

2. **Proof of Loss**
   a. The Insured shall file a proof of loss within 60 days after the date of loss, unless such time is extended in writing by Nexus Insurance Company.

3. **Governmental Action**
   a. Any loss caused by flood resulting from governmental action or order to prevent or mitigate flood damage is excluded.

4. **Concurrent Causation**
   a. If flood is the efficient proximate cause of loss, coverage applies. If flood is a concurrent cause with an excluded peril, coverage does not apply.

5. **Exclusions**
   a. This Endorsement does not insure:
      i. Loss caused by water that backs up through sewers or drains;
      ii. Loss caused by water below the surface of the ground, including water in a basement or crawl space; or
      iii. Loss caused by seepage or leakage of water through a foundation or retaining wall.

**C. Definitions**
   a. "Flood" is defined in Section A.1.a.ii of this Endorsement.

**D. Effective Date and Premium**
   a. This Endorsement is effective at 12:01 a.m., standard time, at the Insured location on the date shown on the Declarations Page, subject to payment of the required premium.

**E. Cancellation**
   a. This Endorsement may be canceled by Nexus Insurance Company or the Insured in accordance with the Policy provisions.
