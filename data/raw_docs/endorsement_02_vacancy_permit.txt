**VACANCY PERMIT ENDORSEMENT**

In consideration of the additional premium charged, the provisions of Section 7.3, *Vacancy Exclusion*, of the base policy are hereby modified as follows:

**1. Modification to Section 7.3 – Vacancy Exclusion**

a. **Extension of Vacancy Period**
The sixty (60) consecutive day vacancy period referenced in Section 7.3 is extended to one hundred twenty (120) consecutive days. This extension applies solely to the vacancy exclusion and does not alter any other provisions of the base policy.

b. **Notification Requirement**
The Insured shall provide written notice to the Company within ten (10) days of the property becoming vacant. Failure to provide such notice shall void the modification set forth in Subsection 1.a. above.

c. **Preservation of Other Terms**
All other terms, conditions, exclusions, and limitations of Section 7.3 remain unchanged and continue to apply in full force and effect, except as expressly modified herein.

**2. Definitions**

For purposes of this endorsement, the term “vacant” shall retain the meaning assigned in Section 7.3 of the base policy, unless otherwise modified by this endorsement.

**3. Premium Adjustment**

The additional premium for this endorsement is calculated based on the risk exposure associated with the extended vacancy period and is due upon attachment of this endorsement to the policy.

**4. Severability**

If any provision of this endorsement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remainder of the endorsement shall remain in full force and effect.

**5. Entire Agreement**

This endorsement constitutes the entire agreement between the Insured and Nexus Insurance Company regarding the modification of the vacancy exclusion and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous agreements or representations.

**6. Policy Integration**

This endorsement is incorporated into and forms a part of the policy to which it is attached. In the event of any conflict between this endorsement and the base policy, the terms of this endorsement shall control.

**7. Effective Date**

This endorsement is effective as of the date of attachment, as shown in the policy declarations, and shall remain in force until the expiration or cancellation of the policy.
