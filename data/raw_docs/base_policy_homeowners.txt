**NEXUS HOMEOWNERS INSURANCE POLICY**
**Policy Form NX-HO-3**

---

### **AGREEMENT**

1. **Insuring Agreement.** In consideration of the premium and the statements made in the Declarations, Nexus Insurance Company ("We," "Our," or "Us") agrees with the Insured named in the Declarations to provide the insurance described in this Policy, subject to all of the terms, exclusions, conditions, and other provisions of this Policy.

---

### **DEFINITIONS**

2. **Definitions.** The following words and phrases shall have the meanings given them in this Policy:

   2.1. **"Insured"** means:
      (a) The named Insured as stated in the Declarations;
      (b) The relatives of the named Insured who reside with the named Insured;
      (c) Persons under the age of 21 who are in the care of the named Insured or of a relative of the named Insured who resides with the named Insured;
      (d) Full-time students who are relatives of the named Insured or who are under the age of 24 and in the care of such relatives, provided such students are enrolled in an educational institution and were residents of the household before moving out to attend school; and
      (e) With respect to the Section II Liability Coverages, any person or organization legally responsible for animals or watercraft owned by an Insured, other than a person or organization in the business of selling, servicing, repairing, storing, or parking such animals or watercraft.

   2.2. **"Residence Premises"** means:
      (a) The one-, two-, three-, or four-family dwelling where the named Insured resides and which is shown as the "residence premises" in the Declarations, including other structures and grounds at that location; or
      (b) That part of any other building where the named Insured resides; or
      (c) Any premises used by the named Insured in connection with the premises described in (a) or (b) above, including structures, private garages, and grounds.

   2.3. **"Occurrence"** means an accident, including continuous or repeated exposure to substantially the same general harmful conditions, which results in bodily injury or property damage during the policy period.

   2.4. **"Vacancy"** means a dwelling that is not occupied by the named Insured or by any other person or persons regularly residing therein for more than 60 consecutive days. A dwelling under construction is not considered vacant.

---

### **SECTION I - PROPERTY COVERAGES**

3. **Coverage A - Dwelling.** We insure against risk of direct physical loss to the dwelling described in the Declarations, including structures attached to such dwelling, and materials and supplies located on or next to the residence premises for use in the construction, alteration, or repair of such dwelling or other structures at the residence premises.

4. **Coverage B - Other Structures.** We insure against risk of direct physical loss to other structures on the residence premises that are separated from the dwelling by clear space. This coverage does not apply to structures rented or held for rental to any person not a tenant of the dwelling, unless used solely as a private garage.

5. **Coverage C - Personal Property.** We insure against risk of direct physical loss to personal property owned or used by an Insured while it is anywhere in the world. At the named Insured’s request, we will cover personal property of others while on the residence premises occupied by the named Insured.

---

### **SECTION I - PERILS INSURED AGAINST**

6. **Perils Insured Against.** We insure for direct physical loss to the property described in Coverages A, B, and C caused by any of the following perils unless the loss is excluded in Section I - Exclusions.

   6.1. **Fire or Lightning.**
   6.2. **Windstorm or Hail.**
   6.3. **Explosion.**
   6.4. **Riot or Civil Commotion.**
   6.5. **Aircraft.**
   6.6. **Vehicles.**
   6.7. **Smoke.**
   6.8. **Vandalism or Malicious Mischief.**
   6.9. **Theft.**
   6.10. **Falling Objects.**
   6.11. **Weight of Ice, Snow, or Sleet.**
   6.12. **Accidental Discharge or Overflow of Water or Steam.**
   6.13. **Sudden and Accidental Tearing Apart, Cracking, Burning, or Bulging.**
   6.14. **Freezing.**
   6.15. **Sudden and Accidental Damage from Artificially Generated Electrical Current.**
   6.16. **Volcanic Eruption.**

---

### **SECTION I - EXCLUSIONS**

7. **Exclusions.** We do not insure for loss caused by any of the following. However, any ensuing loss to property described in Coverages A and B not excluded or excepted in this section is covered.

   7.1. **Ordinance or Law.** Loss arising from the enforcement of any ordinance or law regulating the construction, repair, or demolition of a building or structure.

   7.2. **Earth Movement.** Loss caused by or resulting from earth movement, including but not limited to earthquake, landslide, mudslide, sinkhole, or any volcanic activity.

   7.3. **Vacancy.** We do not insure for loss on the residence premises if the dwelling has been vacant for more than 60 consecutive days immediately before the loss. This exclusion applies to:
      (a) vandalism and malicious mischief;
      (b) glass breakage;
      (c) water damage from frozen pipes.
      A dwelling under construction is not considered vacant.

   7.4. **Water Damage.** Loss caused by or resulting from:
      (a) flood, surface water, waves, tidal water, overflow of a body of water, or spray from any of these, whether or not driven by wind;
      (b) water or water-borne material which backs up through sewers or drains or which overflows from a sump;
      (c) water or water-borne material below the surface of the ground, including water which exerts pressure on or seeps or leaks through a building, sidewalk, driveway, foundation, swimming pool, or other structure.

   7.5. **Power Failure.** Loss caused by or resulting from the failure of power or other utility service if the failure occurs away from the residence premises.

   7.6. **Neglect.** Loss caused by or resulting from the Insured’s neglect to use all reasonable means to save and preserve property at and after the time of loss.

   7.7. **War.** Loss caused by or resulting from war, including undeclared war, civil war, insurrection, rebellion, or revolution.

   7.8. **Nuclear Hazard.** Loss caused by or resulting from nuclear reaction, radiation, or radioactive contamination, however caused.

   7.9. **Intentional Loss.** Loss arising out of any act committed by or at the direction of an Insured with the intent to cause a loss.

   7.10. **Governmental Action.** Loss caused by or resulting from the destruction, confiscation, or seizure of property by order of any governmental or public authority.

   7.11. **Loss to Property.** Loss to property described in Coverage C caused by:
      (a) wear and tear;
      (b) marring, scratching, or denting;
      (c) inherent vice;
      (d) mechanical breakdown;
      (e) smog, rust, or other corrosion;
      (f) smoke from agricultural smudging or industrial operations.

---

### **SECTION I - CONDITIONS**

8. **Insurable Interest and Limit of Liability.** Even if more than one person has an insurable interest in the property covered, we will not be liable in any one loss:
   (a) to pay more than the actual cash value of the loss or the applicable limit of liability shown in the Declarations for such loss, whichever is less; or
   (b) to pay more than our share of the loss when other insurance covering the same property is in effect.

9. **Duties After Loss.** The Insured must:
   (a) give prompt notice to Us of the loss;
   (b) notify the police in case of loss by theft;
   (c) protect the property from further damage and keep an accurate record of repair expenses;
   (d) cooperate with Us in the investigation and settlement of the claim;
   (e) prepare an inventory of damaged personal property showing the quantity, description, actual cash value, and amount of loss;
   (f) submit to examination under oath, if requested;
   (g) produce for examination all pertinent records and documents in the Insured’s possession.

10. **Loss Settlement.** We will settle losses to:
    (a) Coverage A and B on the basis of replacement cost without deduction for depreciation, subject to the following:
       (i) The Insured must repair or replace the damaged property within two years after the date of loss;
       (ii) The amount we will pay for loss to such property is the lesser of:
           (A) the limit of liability shown in the Declarations for such property;
           (B) the replacement cost of such property; or
           (C) the amount actually spent to repair or replace such property.
    (b) Coverage C on the basis of actual cash value at the time of loss.

11. **Other Insurance.** If other insurance applies to a loss covered by this Policy, we will pay only the proportion of the loss that the limit of liability under this Policy bears to the total amount of insurance covering the loss.

12. **Suit Against Us.** No action can be brought against Us unless the Insured has complied with all of the terms of this Policy and the action is started within two years after the date of loss.

---

### **SECTION II - LIABILITY COVERAGES**

13. **Coverage E - Personal Liability.** We cover the Insured for claims or suits brought against an Insured for damages because of bodily injury or property damage caused by an occurrence to which this coverage applies. We will pay up to our limit of liability for the damages for which the Insured is legally liable. We will also pay all reasonable expenses we incur.

14. **Coverage F - Medical Payments to Others.** We will pay the necessary medical expenses that are incurred or medically determined within three years from the date of an accident causing bodily injury. Medical expenses means reasonable charges for medical, surgical, x-ray, dental, ambulance, hospital, professional nursing, and funeral services.

15. **Exclusions Applicable to Coverage E and F.** Coverage E and F do not apply to:
    (a) bodily injury or property damage which is expected or intended by the Insured;
    (b) bodily injury or property damage arising out of business pursuits of an Insured or the rental of any premises;
    (c) bodily injury or property damage arising out of the ownership, maintenance, use, loading, unloading, entrustment to others, or existence of any aircraft, motor vehicle, or watercraft owned or operated by or rented or loaned to an Insured;
    (d) bodily injury or property damage arising out of the transmission of a communicable disease by an Insured;
    (e) bodily injury or property damage arising out of sexual molestation, corporal punishment, or physical or mental abuse;
    (f) bodily injury or property damage arising out of the use, sale, manufacture, delivery, transfer, or possession of a controlled substance.

16. **Additional Coverages.** We also cover:
    (a) Claim Expenses. We will pay, in addition to our limit of liability, all expenses we incur and all costs taxed against an Insured in any suit we defend.
    (b) First Aid Expenses. We will pay expenses for first aid to others incurred by an Insured for bodily injury covered under this Policy.
    (c) Damage to Property of Others. We will pay up to $1,000 per occurrence for property damage caused by an Insured.

17. **Conditions Applicable to Coverage E and F.**
    (a) The Insured must promptly notify Us of any occurrence or offense which may result in a claim or suit.
    (b) The Insured must immediately forward to Us every notice, demand, summons, or other process received concerning the occurrence.
    (c) The Insured must cooperate with Us in the investigation and settlement of any claim or suit.
    (d) No action can be brought against Us unless the Insured has complied with all of the terms of this Policy.
