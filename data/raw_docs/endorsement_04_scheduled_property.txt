### **2. COVERAGE PROVISIONS**
**2.1 Coverage A – Scheduled Property**
Each scheduled item is insured under **Coverage A – Scheduled Property** for the full coverage limit stated in the schedule, subject to the following:
(a) The items are covered for **all risks of direct physical loss** except as excluded in **Section I – Exclusions** of the base policy.
(b) Coverage applies worldwide, including while in transit.
(c) The insurer shall not be liable for loss caused by inherent vice, gradual deterioration, or mechanical breakdown unless resulting from a covered peril.

**2.2 Appraisal Requirement**
(a) For items valued at $5,000 or more, the Named Insured must provide a professional appraisal upon request by the insurer.
(b) The insurer reserves the right to require an updated appraisal every **three (3) years** for scheduled items.

**2.3 Claims Settlement**
(a) In the event of a covered loss, the insurer’s liability shall not exceed the lesser of:
   (i) The cost to repair or replace the item with like kind and quality; or
   (ii) The scheduled coverage limit.
(b) The insurer may elect to pay the actual cash value of the item if repair or replacement is not feasible.

### **3. EXCLUSIONS**
This endorsement does not cover:
(a) Mysterious disappearance unless specifically endorsed.
(b) Loss caused by war, nuclear hazard, or intentional acts by the Named Insured.
(c) Wear and tear, inherent vice, or mechanical breakdown (unless resulting from a covered peril).

### **4. CONDITIONS**
**4.1 Duties After Loss**
The Named Insured must comply with all duties outlined in **Section I – Conditions** of the base policy, including prompt notice of loss and submission of proof of loss.

**4.2 Cancellation**
This endorsement may be canceled by the insurer or Named Insured with **ten (10) days’ written notice**.

**4.3 Premium Adjustment**
The premium for this endorsement is based on the scheduled items and is subject to adjustment upon renewal.
