**NEXUS INSURANCE COMPANY**
**RENTERS INSURANCE POLICY**
**POLICY FORM NX-HO-4**

---

### **AGREEMENT**

**1. Agreement To Insure**
This policy, including the Declarations Page and any attached endorsements, constitutes the entire contract between Nexus Insurance Company ("We," "Us," or "Our") and the Insured named in the Declarations Page ("You" or "Your"). In consideration of the premium paid and the statements made in the application, We agree to provide the insurance coverage as stated in this policy, subject to all its terms, conditions, and exclusions.

---

### **DEFINITIONS**

**2. Definitions**
When used in this policy, the following terms shall have the meanings set forth below:

**2.1 "Insured"**
(a) You and residents of your household who are:
   (i) Your relatives; or
   (ii) Other persons under the age of 21 in the care of any such person.
(b) With respect to Section II Liability Coverage, any person or organization legally responsible for your animals or watercraft to the extent that coverage is afforded under this policy. However, this does not include a person or organization using or having custody of such animals or watercraft in the course of any business or without your permission.

**2.2 "Insured Location"**
(a) The residence premises shown in the Declarations Page.
(b) Any other premises where You temporarily reside, provided that You have no ownership interest in such premises and the premises are not owned by a corporation in which You or any of Your relatives are executive officers, directors, or shareholders.
(c) Any premises You acquire during the policy period, provided that You notify Us within 30 days of such acquisition and We agree to insure such premises under this policy.

**2.3 "Residence Employee"**
An employee of the Insured whose duties are related to the maintenance or use of the residence premises. This includes persons who perform household or domestic services, but does not include persons employed in the course of any business or profession conducted by the Insured.

**2.4 "Occurrence"**
An accident, including continuous or repeated exposure to substantially the same general harmful conditions, which results in bodily injury or property damage during the policy period.

---

### **SECTION I – PERSONAL PROPERTY COVERAGE**

**3. Coverage A – Personal Property**
We will pay for direct physical loss to Your Personal Property caused by a peril insured against, subject to the policy limits and exclusions.

**3.1 Perils Insured Against**
Personal Property is insured against all risks of direct physical loss to the property, except as excluded in Section III Exclusions.

**3.2 Limits of Liability**
(a) The limit of liability for Coverage A is as stated in the Declarations Page.
(b) Special limits apply to certain types of property as follows:
   (i) $200 for money, bank notes, bullion, gold, silver, platinum, coins, and medals.
   (ii) $1,500 for securities, accounts, deeds, evidences of debt, letters of credit, notes other than bank notes, manuscripts, passports, tickets, and stamps.
   (iii) $1,500 for watercraft, including their trailers, furnishings, equipment, and outboard motors.
   (iv) $1,500 for trailers not used with watercraft.
   (v) $2,500 for firearms and related equipment.
   (vi) $2,500 for silverware, goldware, pewterware, and their plated wares.
   (vii) $2,500 for business property on the residence premises.
   (viii) $500 for business property away from the residence premises.
   (ix) $1,500 for electronic apparatus and accessories while in or upon a motor vehicle.

**3.3 Deductible**
The amount shown in the Declarations Page will be deducted from any loss payable under Coverage A.

---

### **SECTION II – LIABILITY**

**4. Coverage E – Personal Liability**
We will pay up to the limit of liability for damages for which any Insured is legally liable due to bodily injury or property damage caused by an occurrence to which this coverage applies. We will also pay defense costs in addition to the limit of liability.

**4.1 Limit of Liability**
The limit of liability for Coverage E is as stated in the Declarations Page.

**4.2 Exclusions**
Coverage E does not apply to:
(a) Any liability arising out of the ownership, maintenance, use, loading, or unloading of motor vehicles, aircraft, or watercraft, except:
   (i) A motor vehicle designed for primarily serving the handicapped and parked on the residence premises.
   (ii) A motor vehicle not required to be registered for use on public roads and used solely to service the residence premises or assist the handicapped.
   (iii) A motorized golf cart used on a golf course or within a private residential community.
(b) Any liability arising out of the transmission of a communicable disease by an Insured.
(c) Any liability arising out of business pursuits of an Insured, except for activities for which no insured receives more than $2,000 in total compensation for the 12 months prior to the loss.
(d) Any liability arising out of the rendering or failure to render professional services.
(e) Any liability arising out of the ownership, maintenance, or use of any premises owned by or rented to an Insured that is not an Insured Location.
(f) Any liability arising out of the acts of an Insured which are intended to cause bodily injury or property damage.

**5. Coverage F – Medical Payments To Others**
We will pay the necessary medical expenses incurred within three years from the date of an accident causing bodily injury to:
(a) A person on the Insured Location with the permission of an Insured.
(b) A person off the Insured Location if the bodily injury arises out of a condition on the Insured Location or the immediate surroundings.

**5.1 Limit of Liability**
The limit of liability for Coverage F is as stated in the Declarations Page.

**5.2 Exclusions**
Coverage F does not apply to:
(a) Bodily injury to any person eligible to receive benefits under any workers’ compensation law.
(b) Bodily injury to an Insured or any regular resident of the residence premises, except residence employees.
(c) Bodily injury arising out of the transmission of a communicable disease by an Insured.

---
### **SECTION III – EXCLUSIONS**

**6. Exclusions Applicable To Section I – Personal Property**
We do not insure for loss caused directly or indirectly by any of the following:
(a) Ordinance Or Law, meaning enforcement of any ordinance or law regulating the construction, repair, or demolition of a building or structure.
(b) Earth Movement, meaning earthquake, landslide, mudslide, or any other earth movement.
(c) Water Damage, meaning flood, surface water, waves, tidal water, overflow of a body of water, or water or water-borne material that backs up through sewers or drains.
(d) Power Failure, meaning the failure of power or other utility service if the failure occurs away from the residence premises.
(e) Neglect, meaning neglect of the Insured to use all reasonable means to save and preserve property at and after the time of loss.
(f) War, including the discharge of a nuclear weapon, and any consequences of such discharge.
(g) Nuclear Hazard, meaning any nuclear reaction, radiation, or radioactive contamination.
(h) Intentional Loss, meaning loss arising out of any act committed by or at the direction of an Insured with the intent to cause a loss.
(i) Governmental Action, meaning the destruction, confiscation, or seizure of property by any governmental or public authority.

**7. Exclusions Applicable To Section II – Liability**
We do not insure for:
(a) Expected Or Intended Injury, meaning bodily injury or property damage which is expected or intended by an Insured.
(b) Business Activities, meaning bodily injury or property damage arising out of the conduct of a business by an Insured.
(c) Professional Services, meaning bodily injury or property damage arising out of the rendering or failure to render professional services.
(d) Communicable Disease, meaning bodily injury or property damage arising out of the transmission of a communicable disease by an Insured.
(e) Motor Vehicles, meaning bodily injury or property damage arising out of the ownership, maintenance, use, loading, or unloading of motor vehicles, except as provided in Section 4.2(a).

---
### **SECTION IV – CONDITIONS**

**8. Duties After Loss**
In the event of a loss, the Insured shall:
(a) Give prompt notice to Us of the loss, including the time, place, and circumstances of the occurrence.
(b) Notify the police in case of loss by theft.
(c) Protect the property from further damage and keep an accurate record of repair expenses.
(d) Cooperate with Us in the investigation and settlement of the claim.
(e) Prepare an inventory of damaged personal property showing the quantity, description, and amount of loss.
(f) Submit to examination under oath, if requested by Us.

**9. Loss Settlement**
(a) Personal Property losses will be settled at actual cash value, unless otherwise specified in the policy.
(b) We may repair or replace any damaged property with material of like kind and quality.

**10. Other Insurance**
If other insurance applies to a loss covered under this policy, We will not pay more than the proportion of the loss that the limit of liability under this policy bears to the total amount of insurance covering the loss.

**11. Cancellation**
This policy may be canceled by You or Us as permitted by law. If We cancel, We will provide at least 30 days’ notice.

**12. Assignment**
Assignment of this policy without Our written consent is invalid.

**13. Subrogation**
Any rights of recovery the Insured may have against another party shall be transferred to Us to the extent We pay the loss.
