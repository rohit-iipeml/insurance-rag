**EARTHQUAKE COVERAGE ENDORSEMENT**

This endorsement amends the policy referenced above to add coverage for loss caused by earthquake, as defined herein. All terms, conditions, and exclusions of the policy remain in full force and effect except as modified by this endorsement.

**SECTION I – PROPERTY COVERAGES**

**A. PERILS INSURED AGAINST**

1. **Earthquake Coverage Added.** The following peril is added to the list of perils insured against under Section I – Coverage A (Dwelling), Coverage B (Other Structures), Coverage C (Personal Property), and Coverage D (Loss of Use):

   a. **Earthquake.** Sudden and accidental shaking of the earth caused by:
      i. **Tectonic activity;**
      ii. **Volcanic eruption;**
      iii. **Land shock waves** resulting from either of the above.

   b. **Definitions.**
      i. **Earthquake** includes all related shocks and tremors occurring within a 168-hour (7-day) period, which shall be deemed a single event for the purpose of applying the deductible.
      ii. **Volcanic eruption** means the explosion, effusion, or emission of molten rock, steam, or gases from a volcano.

2. **Deductible.** A deductible of **10% of the Coverage A (Dwelling) limit** applies to all loss or damage caused by earthquake. This deductible is in lieu of any other deductible provided in the policy.

**B. EXCLUSIONS – OVERRIDDEN**

1. **Section I – Exclusion A.2 (Earth Movement) is hereby overridden** to the extent necessary to provide coverage for loss caused by earthquake as defined in this endorsement. All other exclusions in the policy remain applicable.

**C. LIMITATIONS**

1. **No Coverage for Certain Losses.** This endorsement does not insure against:
   a. **Loss caused by flood, tidal wave, or tsunami**, even if generated by an earthquake;
   b. **Land subsidence, landslide, mudslide, or sinkhole**, unless directly caused by an earthquake as defined herein;
   c. **Cost of filling land, grading, or excavating** unless required to restore the dwelling to its pre-loss condition;
   d. **Loss to exterior masonry, parapets, or ornamentation** unless part of a covered structure;
   e. **Loss to awnings, outdoor equipment, or detached structures** unless specifically covered under Section I – Coverage B.

2. **Settlement and Cracking.** Loss caused by gradual settling, cracking, or expansion of the foundation or structure is excluded unless directly resulting from a covered earthquake event.

**D. ADDITIONAL CONDITIONS**

1. **Notice of Claim.** The insured must provide written notice of any earthquake loss to Nexus Insurance Company within **72 hours** of the event or as soon as reasonably possible.

2. **Appraisal.** If the insured and Nexus Insurance Company cannot agree on the amount of loss, either may demand an appraisal in accordance with Section I – Condition 6 of the policy.

3. **Deductible Application.** The earthquake deductible shall be applied to the total loss amount before any other policy deductible.

**E. SEVERABILITY**

The provisions of this endorsement shall apply separately to each insured claiming a loss under this policy.

**F. EFFECTIVE DATE AND DURATION**

This endorsement is effective as of the date shown above and shall remain in force until the policy expires or is canceled in accordance with its terms.
