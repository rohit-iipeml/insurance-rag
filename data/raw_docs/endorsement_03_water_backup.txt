**NEXUS INSURANCE COMPANY**
**HOMEOWNERS POLICY ENDORSEMENT**
**FORM NX-END-03**
**WATER BACKUP AND SUMP OVERFLOW COVERAGE**

**1. ADDITIONAL COVERAGE — WATER BACKUP AND SUMP OVERFLOW**

a. **Insuring Agreement.** Nexus Insurance Company agrees, subject to the terms, conditions, and limitations herein, to pay for direct physical loss to property insured under Section I of the Policy caused by water that:
   (1) backs up through sewers or drains; or
   (2) overflows or is discharged from a sump, sump pump, or related equipment designed to remove subsurface water from a building.

b. **Sublimit.** The Company’s liability for loss under this Endorsement shall not exceed **$10,000** per occurrence, regardless of the number of sump pumps, sewers, drains, or insured locations involved.

**2. EXCLUSIONS**

This Endorsement does not apply to loss caused by:
a. **Neglect.** The failure to repair or replace a sump pump or related equipment within a reasonable time after becoming aware of its inoperability.
b. **Sewer or Drain Backup Not from a Covered Cause.** Water backup that occurs due to the intentional or negligent act of any person or entity not insured under this Policy.
c. **Gradual Damage.** Loss caused by continuous or repeated seepage or leakage of water, unless such seepage or leakage results from a sudden and accidental occurrence covered under this Endorsement.
d. **Governmental Action.** Loss caused by or resulting from the order of any governmental or public authority.

**3. LIMITATIONS**

a. **Deductible.** The applicable deductible under Section I of the Policy shall apply to any loss covered under this Endorsement.
b. **Concurrent Causation.** If loss is caused by a peril covered under this Endorsement and a peril excluded under Section I, Exclusion A.3.b of the Policy, the Company shall not pay for the portion of the loss attributable to the excluded peril.
c. **Prevention of Further Damage.** The insured shall use all reasonable means to prevent further damage to the property once the loss is known to the insured.

**4. POLICY MODIFICATION**

This Endorsement modifies and is made a part of the Policy. In the event of any conflict between this Endorsement and the Policy, the terms of this Endorsement shall control.

**5. REFERENCE TO POLICY EXCLUSIONS**

Section I, Exclusion A.3.b of the Policy is hereby partially overridden to the extent necessary to provide coverage for loss caused by water backup through sewers or drains or sump pump overflow as described in this Endorsement.
