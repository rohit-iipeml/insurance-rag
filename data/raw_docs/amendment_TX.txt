**NEXUS INSURANCE COMPANY**
**HOMEOWNERS POLICY – TEXAS AMENDATORY ENDORSEMENT**
**Form NX-TX-AMD**

This endorsement amends the policy to which it is attached, issued by Nexus Insurance Company, in accordance with the requirements of the Texas Department of Insurance and the Texas Insurance Code. The terms of this endorsement shall control in the event of any conflict with the policy to which it is attached.

**SECTION 1 – WIND AND HAIL DEDUCTIBLE**

1.1 **Deductible Applicable to Wind and Hail Losses.** In the event of loss or damage caused by windstorm or hail, the deductible applicable to such loss shall be separate from and in addition to any other deductible provided under the policy. The deductible for windstorm or hail losses shall be two percent (2%) of the limit of liability under Coverage A – Dwelling.

1.2 **Calculation of Deductible.** The deductible shall be calculated as follows:
   a. The deductible amount shall be two percent (2%) of the Coverage A limit stated in the Declarations.
   b. The deductible shall apply on a per occurrence basis.
   c. The deductible shall not be subject to any other deductible or limitation under the policy.

1.3 **Application of Deductible.** The deductible shall apply to all loss or damage caused by windstorm or hail, including but not limited to damage to the dwelling, other structures, and personal property.

**SECTION 2 – FLOOD EXCLUSION AND DISCLAIMER**

2.1 **Flood Exclusion.** Loss or damage caused directly or indirectly by flood is excluded from coverage under the policy. For purposes of this endorsement, "flood" means:
   a. A general and temporary condition of partial or complete inundation of normally dry land areas from:
      i. The overflow of inland or tidal waters;
      ii. The unusual and rapid accumulation or runoff of surface waters from any source; or
      iii. Mudslides or mudflows caused by flooding.
   b. Collapse or subsidence of land along the shore of a lake or similar body of water as a result of erosion or undermining caused by waves or currents of water exceeding anticipated cyclical levels.

2.2 **NFIP Coverage Available.** Flood coverage may be available through the National Flood Insurance Program (NFIP). Policyholders are encouraged to contact their insurance agent or visit www.floodsmart.gov for more information regarding flood insurance coverage.

**SECTION 3 – TEXAS HOMEOWNERS BILL OF RIGHTS DISCLOSURE**

3.1 **Texas Homeowners Bill of Rights.** Pursuant to Texas Insurance Code § 542A.003, Nexus Insurance Company provides the following disclosure to policyholders regarding their rights under the Texas Homeowners Bill of Rights:
   a. **Prompt Response to Claims.** Nexus Insurance Company will promptly acknowledge and respond to all communications regarding claims made under the policy.
   b. **Fair and Equitable Settlement.** Nexus Insurance Company will provide a fair and equitable settlement of all claims in accordance with the terms and conditions of the policy.
   c. **Denial of Claims.** In the event of a denial of a claim, Nexus Insurance Company will provide a written explanation of the basis for the denial, in accordance with Texas Insurance Code § 542A.007.
   d. **Right to Appeal.** Policyholders have the right to appeal any denial of a claim to the Texas Department of Insurance, in accordance with Texas Insurance Code § 542A.008.
   e. **Prohibited Practices.** Nexus Insurance Company is prohibited from engaging in unfair or deceptive practices, in accordance with Texas Insurance Code § 542A.009.
