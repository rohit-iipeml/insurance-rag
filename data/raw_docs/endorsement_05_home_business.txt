**NEXUS INSURANCE COMPANY**
**HOME BUSINESS LIABILITY ENDORSEMENT**
**ENDORSEMENT FORM NX-END-05**

This endorsement, designated as **NX-END-05**, amends the policy identified in the Declarations by adding coverage for certain home-based business activities as described herein. This endorsement is made a part of the policy to which it is attached.

**1. Coverage Provision**
a. **Extension of Coverage E – Personal Liability**: Notwithstanding any provision of the policy to the contrary, Coverage E – Personal Liability is extended to include liability arising out of the conduct of a home-based business operated by an "insured," provided that:
   i. The home-based business is conducted on the "residence premises" described in the Declarations;
   ii. The gross annual receipts from the home-based business do not exceed **$25,000**;
   iii. The home-based business is not engaged in the sale of products or services that are:
      (1) Wholesale;
      (2) Manufacturing;
      (3) Automobile repair or service;
      (4) Financial services (including tax preparation, bookkeeping, or insurance services);
      (5) Medical or dental services;
      (6) Professional services requiring a license (e.g., legal, architectural, or engineering services); or
      (7) Any other business operation deemed hazardous by Nexus Insurance Company.
   iv. The home-based business does not involve the rental of the "residence premises" or any portion thereof to others;
   v. The home-based business does not involve the use of commercial vehicles or the storage of business property exceeding **$10,000** in value on the "residence premises."

b. **Liability Limit**: The limit of liability for Coverage E – Personal Liability arising out of home-based business activities under this endorsement is **$300,000** per occurrence. This limit is in addition to the limit stated in the Declarations for Coverage E.

**2. Exclusions**
a. This endorsement does not apply to:
   i. **Bodily injury** or **property damage** arising out of the rendering or failure to render professional services, including but not limited to medical, dental, legal, or accounting services;
   ii. **Bodily injury** or **property damage** arising out of the sale, distribution, or manufacture of products, including food products;
   iii. **Bodily injury** or **property damage** arising out of the ownership, maintenance, or use of motor vehicles, aircraft, or watercraft, except as provided in Section I of the policy;
   iv. **Bodily injury** or **property damage** arising out of the rental of the "residence premises" or any portion thereof to others;
   v. **Bodily injury** or **property damage** arising out of the conduct of any business not described in Section 1.a. above;
   vi. **Bodily injury** or **property damage** for which the "insured" is covered under a workers' compensation, disability benefits, or similar law;
   vii. **Bodily injury** or **property damage** arising out of the failure of any product or service sold, distributed, or manufactured by the home-based business to perform as intended or warranted.

**3. Definitions**
a. **"Home-based business"** means a business operated by an "insured" from the "residence premises," which is not required to be separately zoned for commercial use.
b. **"Insured"** means the named insured and residents of the household who are relatives or under the age of 21 in the care of the named insured.
c. **"Residence premises"** means the one-, two-, three-, or four-family dwelling where the named insured resides and which is shown in the Declarations.

**4. Policy Terms Unaffected**
a. All other terms, conditions, exclusions, and definitions of the policy remain unchanged except as specifically amended by this endorsement.

**5. Effective Date and Premium**
a. This endorsement is effective as of the date shown in the Declarations and is subject to the payment of the applicable premium.
