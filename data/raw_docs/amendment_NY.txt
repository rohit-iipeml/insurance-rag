**RE: AMENDMENTS TO HOMEOWNERS INSURANCE POLICY(S) ISSUED IN THE STATE OF NEW YORK**

Pursuant to the requirements of the New York Department of Financial Services and in accordance with New York Insurance Law § 3102 (Disclosure of Policy Provisions), § 3425 (Property/Casualty Insurance Rates, Premiums, Classifications and Policy Forms), and § 2304 (Unfair Methods of Competition and Unfair and Deceptive Acts and Practices), this Amendatory Endorsement modifies the terms of the referenced Homeowners insurance policy(s) issued by Nexus Insurance Company in the State of New York.

**1. MOLD COVERAGE DISCLOSURE (NEW YORK INSURANCE LAW § 3102)**
1.1. **Exclusion of Mold Coverage.** Section [X] of the policy, entitled "Exclusions," is hereby amended to include the following additional exclusion:
   1.1.1. **Mold, Fungus, or Wet Rot.** Loss caused by, resulting from, or consisting of mold, fungus, mildew, wet rot, dry rot, or bacteria, regardless of whether such loss is caused by a peril insured against under the policy, is excluded from coverage under Section I of the policy.

1.2. **Written Notice of Exclusion.** The insurer has provided written notice of this exclusion to the Named Insured(s) in accordance with New York Insurance Law § 3102, which includes:
   1.2.1. A clear and conspicuous disclosure of the mold exclusion;
   1.2.2. A statement that the policy does not cover loss caused by mold, fungus, or wet rot; and
   1.2.3. A recommendation that the Named Insured(s) consider obtaining additional coverage for mold-related losses, if desired.

**2. TENANT PROTECTIONS FOR RENTERS POLICIES (NEW YORK INSURANCE LAW § 3425)**
2.1. **Notice of Cancellation or Nonrenewal.** In the event the policy is a renters insurance policy, Section [Y] of the policy, entitled "Cancellation and Nonrenewal," is hereby amended to include the following provisions:
   2.1.1. **Extended Notice Period.** The insurer shall provide the Named Insured(s) with at least thirty (30) days' written notice prior to cancellation or nonrenewal of the policy, in accordance with New York Insurance Law § 3425(d).
   2.1.2. **Specific Reason for Cancellation or Nonrenewal.** The notice of cancellation or nonrenewal shall include a specific reason for such action, as required by New York Insurance Law § 3425(e).

**3. ANTI-ARSON APPLICATION REQUIREMENT (NEW YORK INSURANCE LAW § 2304)**
3.1. **Application Disclosure.** The insurer has included in the application for insurance a question regarding the Named Insured(s)'s history of arson-related claims or convictions, as required by New York Insurance Law § 2304(a)(1).
3.2. **Misrepresentation.** Any misrepresentation or omission in the application regarding arson-related claims or convictions may result in the denial of coverage or rescission of the policy, in accordance with New York Insurance Law § 2304(a)(2).

**4. SEVERABILITY**
4.1. If any provision of this Amendatory Endorsement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

**5. GOVERNING LAW**
5.1. This Amendatory Endorsement shall be construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York.
